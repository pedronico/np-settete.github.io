Nicola Pedroni

Nicola Pedroni is a Sound Designer, and Composer rooted in the charming city of Florence.
Specializing in crafting distinctive soundscapes, he gives every game its unique identity, striking the perfect balance between standout style and crystal-clear clarity.
Skilled in designing streamlined, no-nonsense audio systems, Nicola values simplicity and impact above all.
With a strong background in remote work, he expertly manages the full audio pipeline and ensures seamless, enjoyable teamwork with every project.

Skills
